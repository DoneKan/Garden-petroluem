Welcome to Garden Petroluem

Garden Petroleum is the leading provider of LPG (cooking gas) in Uganda, offering cost-effective energy solutions tailored to meet your needs. We specialize in distributing LPG in bulk, with tank capacities ranging from 250kg to 3000kg, ensuring that we cater to diverse industrial and commercial consumption requirements. Renowned for delivering affordable and reliable cooking solutions, we also design and construct custom gas lines for Factories, Hotels, Restuarants, Schools and Hospitals optimizing LPG usage for any operational scale. At Garden Petroleum, we are eager to establish a long-term partnerships with our esteemed customers. Our goal is to provide business solutions that align with your needs through our competitively priced products and service packages. We propose to supply you with premium liquefied petroleum gas (LPG), offering both value and innovation in our services.
